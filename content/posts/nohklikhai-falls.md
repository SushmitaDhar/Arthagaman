### Beyond the Edge

There is a specific kind of roar that only a waterfall like **Nohkalikai** can produce. It isn't just the sound of water; it’s the sound of gravity meeting the ancient soul of the Khasi Hills. 

Standing at the viewpoint, watching the water plunge into the turquoise pool below, you realize how small our daily worries are compared to the timeless rhythm of these cliffs.

### Finding Meaning in the Plunge

In the spirit of **Arthagaman**, Nohkalikai represents the courage to dive deep. Often, we stay at the surface of our journeys, but here, the land itself forces you to look down into the depths. 

It is more than just the tallest plunge waterfall in India; it is a reminder that even the most forceful descent can result in something remarkably beautiful.
