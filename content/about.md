### Why Arthagaman?

India is often described through its noise — the bustling markets, the vibrant festivals, the constant movement. But there is another India that exists in the quiet gaps. 

**Arthagaman** is my attempt to find that silence. The name itself reflects a "Journey towards Meaning." 

I started this project because I wanted to capture the moments that usually go unnoticed: the way the light hits the grass in Sohra after a rain, the stories told by elders in remote villages, and the timeless peace of our natural landscapes.

### The Purpose

The goal here is simple: **To slow down.** I want to share the one story at a time. This isn't just a travel blog; it’s a digital sanctuary for anyone who believes that traveling is as much about the inner journey as it is about the destination.

**I’m glad you’re here. Let's wander together.**
